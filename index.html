Our Story

JoJo's Matcha began as a late-night study session staple—a whisked cup to steady the mind. All of our matcha is measured, sifted, and whisked to order; milk options include oat and whole milk. Specials rotate seasonally.

Sourcing

We prioritize responsibly-grown matcha from small suppliers and use organic milk alternatives when possible.

Community

From study breaks to club events, our pop-ups are a place to recharge and connect.
