JoJo's Matcha

Our Story

JoJo's Matcha began as a late-night study session staple—a whisked cup to steady the mind. All of our matcha is measured, sifted, and whisked to order; milk options include oat and whole milk. Specials rotate seasonally.

Our matcha is sourced directly from Japan and is ceremonial grade, ensuring the highest quality and vibrant flavor. We are a student-run underground business at UTD, bringing small-batch matcha to campus and community events.

Sourcing

We prioritize responsibly-grown matcha from small suppliers and use organic milk alternatives when possible.

Community

From study breaks to club events, our pop-ups are a place to recharge and connect.

Ceremonial Quality

We source premium culinary & ceremonial matcha. Light, bright, and naturally vibrant.

Small Batches

Everything is whisked fresh — no syrups or nonsense, except where noted (vanilla/agave options).
